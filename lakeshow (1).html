New to the Lakers?

Where to Start

Never watched the NBA before? No problem. Here's everything you need to go from zero to LakeShow in five steps.

01

Understand the Game

The NBA is 5-on-5 basketball. Four 12-minute quarters, shot clock at 24 seconds. The Lakers play in the Western Conference — 82 games a season, then playoffs.

02

Know the History

17 NBA Championships. Kobe. Magic. Kareem. LeBron. The most decorated franchise in the modern era — but always chasing the next ring.

03

Pick Your Player

Every Lakers fan has their guy. LeBron for the culture? Anthony Davis for the highlight reel? Bronny for the next generation? Use our picker below.

04

Watch in the UK

Most games tip off between 00:30 and 03:30 GMT. Sky Sports, TNT Sports, and NBA League Pass International all cover the Lakers. Use our time converter above.

05

Join the Community

Tag #LakeShowUK on TikTok or Instagram with your takes, reactions and game-day fits. Get reposted by @Lakers and become part of the UK fan wall.

Meet the Players

Four Lakers worth knowing — and why UK fans connect with each one.

LeBron James

Small Forward

The Legend

The most culturally significant athlete on the planet. LeBron's 'More Than a Vote' campaign, Space Jam 2, and SpringHill Company have made him as relevant in London as in LA. Off the court, he's UK streetwear, grime playlists, and NBA 2K in one person.

Bronny James

Gen Z Icon

Same age as most of our community. Bronny grew up on social media, just like you — and now he's in the NBA. His journey from NBA 2K player to actual Laker is the story that resonates hardest with UK Gen Z fans discovering the sport through gaming culture.

Anthony Davis

Centre / Power Forward

The Brow

When AD is locked in, he's the most dominant player in the league — full stop. 7-foot wingspan, 30-point nights, and the ability to guard every position. If you want highlight reels and rim-protecting blocks that break the internet, this is your guy.

12.6RPG

77

Luka Dončić

Point Guard

The Global Icon

European royalty in purple and gold. The Slovenian superstar arrived in LA via the most shocking trade in NBA history — and is now the league's leading scorer. Step-back threes, slow-mo crossovers and that ice-cold stare. For UK fans, Luka is proof that the world's best basketball isn't just an American game anymore.
